CreshChat v0.3.26 window-flow design
======================================

Main console
- Friends remains first, followed by Whispers, Guild, General, Quests and Combat.
- Games remains a header control that opens a full-height right-side drawer.
- Leaderboards and History are available from compact H and LB drawer controls and from the Solo hub.

Solo arcade
- Six cards fit in two rows of three.
- Higher or Lower follows the same persistent-bank presentation as Hold'em and Blackjack.
- Leaderboard and History overlays remain within the Solo window and use compact, subtle row boxes.
- W/L/D summary boxes are intentionally low-profile and do not compete with game controls.

Chess
- Piece notation remains readable without pretending to be art assets.
- Each piece label sits inside a 30 x 30 token, leaving visible board-square margins.
- White and Black use inverse fill/text treatments for immediate side recognition.

ZLR Arena
- q3a is the theme's baseline launcher identity.
- Notification state is temporary and always resolves through the theme-aware baseline rather than a hard-coded C.
- The composer prompt uses /say or /console to reinforce the arena-console style.

Shared scores
- No public/global service is required.
- Compatible players exchange compact records through private addon messages when discovered.
- Cached remote rows persist locally until refreshed by a later scan/message.

BATTLE PASS DRAWER
- The existing right-side slide animation now serves four drawer modes: Solo, Multi, Battle Pass and Unlock Themes.
- Battle Pass uses a long scrollable reward track while keeping the drawer height matched to the console.
- Unlock Themes uses compact colour swatches and state-aware buttons: NEED, UNLOCK NOW, EQUIP and EQUIPPED.


v0.3.24 REFINEMENT
- Numerical settings use boxed 48-pixel rows with a plain horizontal track, accent fill and compact thumb.
- The Settings page is intentionally always on top, while the rest of CreshChat retains click-to-front layering.
- Screen-fit scale limiting is a safety override only; the saved requested UI scale remains the user's chosen value.
- Locked Battle Pass rows behave as navigation: clicking one reveals its requirement and a direct game action.
