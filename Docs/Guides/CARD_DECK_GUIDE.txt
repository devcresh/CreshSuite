CreshChat Card Deck Guide

Starter decks
- Classic 8-Bit is always unlocked.
- One of Alliance Vanguard, Horde Warband, Fel Crusade, Shattrath Light, Netherstorm Arcana or Dark Portal is selected as the account/profile random starter deck.

Battle Pass
- Remaining decks are awarded when their configured Battle Pass tier is claimed.
- If the random starter matches a tier reward, that tier retains its normal coins/theme reward and does not award a duplicate deck.

Changing decks
- Open Texas Hold'em, Blackjack or Higher or Lower.
- Left-click DECK to move forward through unlocked decks.
- Right-click DECK to move backward.
- Hold'em selection is shared between solo and multiplayer.
- Blackjack and Higher or Lower store their own selected deck.

Files
- CardDeckLibrary.lua contains texture paths and metadata.
- CardDecks.lua manages ownership, Battle Pass backfill, selection and card rendering.
- Media/Games/Cards contains all faces, backs and deck icons.
