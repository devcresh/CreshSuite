CreshChat v0.3.46 Game Progression

- Every game has persistent level and XP.
- Solo game start: 5 game XP. Multiplayer start: 10 game XP.
- Win 30, draw 20, loss 15; endless runs get 15 plus score bonus. Multiplayer result XP is doubled.
- Every game level awards 10 Cresh Coins and 10 Battle Pass XP.
- Every 1,000 estimated walking steps awards 1 Cresh Coin and 1 Battle Pass XP.
- Battle Pass also contains walking and creature-kill milestone goals.
