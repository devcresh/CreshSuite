CreshChat 0.2-final-audit

- Public version reset to 0.2; SavedVariables schema advanced to 75 without progression reset.
- Responsive header layout verified for normal, Friends and Whisper control sets.
- Settings coverage audit added every live end-user option that previously lacked a control; obsolete legacy-only keys remain hidden.
- Top-level frames use clamping or explicit screen-bound positioning; settings, console, drawer, pop-outs, cards and game windows retain safe scaling.
- Addon-message discovery is paced to 6.7 probes/second, capped at 12 targets and protected by a 15-second cooldown. Automatic login-wide probing was removed.
- Static policy scan found no external networking, executables, obfuscated code, advertisements, donation requests, account automation or client modification.
